Pipe Thermal Growth

Free (unrestrained) axial thermal growth of a pipe run: how much a length grows or shrinks when it moves from installation temperature to operating temperature. Every input and the result have their own unit dropdown, so you can mix and match.

Unrestrained growth only (ΔL = α · L · ΔT). It does not compute restrained thermal load, stress, or flexibility. The material quick-fills are approximate mean coefficients near room temperature; α is temperature-dependent, so for real design use the mean coefficient between installation and design temperature from the applicable code table (e.g. ASME B31.1/B31.3 Appendix).
