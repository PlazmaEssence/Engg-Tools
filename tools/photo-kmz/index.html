Photo → KMZ

Drop in a batch of photos and download a KMZ you can open in Google Earth — each photo becomes a pin on the map, and clicking a pin shows the picture. GPS coordinates are read straight from each photo when present; otherwise, type them in. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
