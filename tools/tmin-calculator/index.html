Pipe Wall Thickness (tmin)

Pressure design thickness for straight pipe under internal pressure, per ASME B31.3 §304.1.2 or B31.4 §403.2.1, plus corrosion allowance and mill under-tolerance.

Engineering aid only, not a substitute for code compliance review. The material quick-fills below are a small, deliberately limited set of well-established, ambient-temperature values — always verify S, E, W, Y, F against the current edition of the applicable code, your project specification, and design temperature before use.
